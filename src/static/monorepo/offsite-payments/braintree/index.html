About Braintree

Braintree enables PayPal and Venmo payments through their hosted payment buttons. Users authenticate directly with PayPal/Venmo and the payment nonce is returned for processing.

Flow Steps
  1. Create pending purchase on Spreedly
  2. Get client_token from response
  3. Initialize SpreedlyBraintree
  4. Mount PayPal and/or Venmo buttons
  5. User clicks button and authorizes
  6. Receive nonce in callback
  7. Confirm transaction with nonce
