Button eligibility

Which buttons render is decided by PayPal's findEligibleMethods at payment time (account, country, currency, device). Guidelines:

  • PayPal — any PayPal-enabled account, any amount always
  • Pay Later — US buyer, USD; Pay in 4 ≥ $30, Pay Monthly ≥ $199 —
  • Venmo — US buyer + US IP, USD, mobile; no full sandbox e2e sandbox-limited
  • PayPal Credit — separate funding source (US/UK); not in default scope optional

About PPCP

PayPal Complete Payments renders PayPal-branded buttons (PayPal, Venmo, Pay Later) via the PayPal JS SDK v6, embedded in Spreedly Checkout through the SpreedlyPPCP class. The amount comes from your product selection.

Flow Steps
  1. Select products → cart total = amount
  2. Proceed → load PayPal v6 + local Spreedly SDK
  3. SpreedlyPPCP.mount() → createInstance + findEligibleMethods
  4. Render eligible buttons for the total
  5. Click → createOrder() → /ppcp/orders
  6. Approve → capture → /ppcp/orders/:id/capture
